Halal food service are prepared by following Islamic dietary laws and regulations which determine what is permissible. According to Islamic guidelines, halal food is safe and not harmfully prepared; it does not contain non-halal and najs ingredients; and it is processed and manufactured using equipment that is not contaminated with things that are najs.
